## Overview
Define the human-facing initiatives experience around a persistent strategic portfolio widget, focused portfolio and milestone views, and direct workflows for shaping long-horizon strategic containers without tool-mirroring commands.

## Capabilities
- capability-initiative-portfolio: Strategic initiative portfolio and milestone management
